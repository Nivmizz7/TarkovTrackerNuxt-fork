# :books: Server Rules & Guidelines
Welcome to the TarkovTracker Community. By remaining in this server, you agree to adhere to the following rules.

## 1. Community Conduct
* **Respect & Civility:** Treat everyone with respect. Harassment, witch-hunting, sexism, racism, hate speech, or toxic behavior is strictly prohibited.
* **No Spam or Self-Promotion:** Do not post server invites, advertisements, or solicitations without staff permission. Unsolicited DMs to members for promotion are prohibited.
* **Safe For Work (SFW) Only:** No age-restricted, obscene, or shock content (nudity, sexual content, extreme violence, etc.).
* **English Preferred:** To assist our moderation team, please keep public chats in English.

## 2. Platform Compliance
* **Discord TOS:** You must abide by the Discord [Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms) and [Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines).
* **Age Requirement:** You must be at least 13 years old (or the relevant legal age in your country) to join.

*If you see something against the rules or feel unsafe, please ping or direct message a Staff member or open a ticket.*
